About Me

Hi! I'm Anastasia, a dedicated videographer, photographer, and video editor with six years of professional experience in the cinema industry and commercials. My journey has allowed me to collaborate with prominent brands like Ferrero, Kinder Bueno, Baileys, Recaro, The Brew Company, SelenaCare, Savencia, and Jumbo, crafting visuals that tell compelling stories and capture the essence of each brand.

My passion for visual storytelling is matched by my technical expertise, which spans drone filming—a skill certified by my drone license—through to mastery of the Adobe Creative Suite, Final Cut Pro, and Sony cameras. My work not only reflects a deep understanding of lighting and color gradients but also extends into product design and Photoshop, enabling me to bring a comprehensive and refined vision to every project.
